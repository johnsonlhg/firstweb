Company Profile

Maxim  Corporation  is a  leading   specialist in textile  accessories,  providing  a
comprehensive range  of high-quality spare  parts and solutions  for the  textile
industry.We supply precision-engineered needles and components   compatible
with a widearray of knitting machines.
With a strong focus on advanced manufacturing  processes, premium  materials,
and cutting-edge technology, we are committed to delivering superior products
at  competitive  prices. Leveraging  our  industry  expertise, we  provide tailored
recommendations to ensure our clients receive the most  suitable  parts  for their
specific needs.
At  Maxim  Corporation,  innovation and  collaboration drive our  commitment  to
excellence. We continuously enhance our product offerings and service capabilities
to  maximize  value  for  our customers. Our  ultimate  goal is  to  foster  long-term
partnerships built on mutual success and shared growth.
